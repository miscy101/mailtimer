⚠ Outlook version notes

Once you click Start, your email is scheduled on the server. Closing Outlook will not cancel the timer. To cancel, use the Cancel button in Mailtimer before the timer reaches zero, or delete the email from your Drafts folder.

The Mailtimer Cancel button will attempt to push the scheduled send far into the future — but if anything goes wrong, check your Drafts folder to be sure.
